ABOUT

Athens Cars & Coffee is a monthly gathering of car, motorcycle and coffee enthusiasts. The meet takes place the first Saturday of each month from 9-11 a.m. at the Beechwood Shopping Center at 196 Alps Rd. Athens, Ga. The event is free and open to everyone. We only ask that you show respect to people and their cars.

LOCATION

The event takes place at the Beechwood Shopping Center in the parking lot between the Fresh Market and Jason's Deli. Parking spaces are first-come first-serve. Athens Cars & Coffee has taken place at various Athens breweries in the past. We'll post any location changes in the Calendar section.
